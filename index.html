UX Design Process

We followed a user-centric design process where users were involved at all stages of the process. Student cyclists firstly helped us to better understand the problem. Later, they provided feedback on our prototypes and participated in usability tests.

The problem

Many students at UCI bike to class or run errands on their bicycle. However, it can be hard to find a safe place to park a bike especially when in an unfamiliar area. Students are only too aware of the high levels of bike theft with over 2 million bicycles stolen in the USA every year.

BikePark is a mobile app that will help students find a secure bicycle park in and around the UCI campus.

Explore Problem

Firstly, to better explore students experiences of cycling at UCI, we interviewed three students who regularly bike to campus. The feedback we received was:

  • Helpful to see bike parks near the user's current location
  • Integration with other map applications such as Google Maps would be helpful
  • Bike safety is important so ratings or reviews of a parking structure would help in deciding how safe a bike park is
